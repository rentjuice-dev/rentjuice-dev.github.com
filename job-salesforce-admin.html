Salesforce.com Administrator

Location: San Francisco, CA Department: Operations Type: Full Time Min. Experience: Mid Level

Send resumes to: 93960-CS-5429@zillow.hrmdirect.com

The RentJuice Marketing Team exists to increase the overall reach of our brand and generate qualified leads for our Inside Sales Team. To achieve these goals, we use Salesforce.com. They help us manage various types of data across all of our prospects, leads, and customers.

Within Salesforce, the Administrator will be responsible for keeping the data of the marketing, sales, and operations team as organized and correct as possible. They will also be responsible for working with the Engineering and Product teams to resolve bugs in the data, expand the depth and detail of data available, and keep the data current with new features as they are deployed.

As an incredibly data-driven organization, the SFDC Administrator is always creating new dashboards and reports that allow other departments to quickly track progress against their goals.

Success in this role means:

  • Designing a system to hold all necessary customer and lead data in a scalable manner
  • Creating dashboards and analytic reports to help different departments track key metrics from our different databases
  • Discovering data inconsistencies and new ways to view it before they are needed/a problem
  • Keeping up with best practices for Salesforce's suites of tools and informing management of new capabilities

Responsibilities:

  • Running ongoing analyses and developing new metrics and analysis by distilling complex raw data into actionable KPIs
  • Assisting team members with creating new dashboards to track effectiveness of their efforts
  • Maintaining the accuracy and comprehensiveness of the data available to the Marketing, Sales, and Operations teams.
  • Responsibilities will increase as our organization continues to scale

What we're looking for:

  • Experience using Salesforce in an administrative capacity
  • Experience with MS Excel and SQL. Like. A. Boss.
  • An understanding of Google Analytics, and other general marketing analytics
  • Finance, consulting, or business analyst background highly sought
  • A track record of striving for continual personal and professional improvement
  • A general understanding of RentJuice's mission and software
