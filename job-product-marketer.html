Product Marketer

Location: San Francisco, CA Department: Marketing Type: Full Time Min. Experience: Mid Level

The RentJuice Marketing Team is responsible for increasing the reach of our brand and generating qualified leads for our Sales Team. Whenever new folks discover RentJuice, whether it's via our website or through a piece of our sales collateral, it's essential that the value of our product is explained as clearly and concisely as possible. This is where a Product Marketer comes in. They know how to articulate the same points to ten different people in such a way that it answers each of their unique sets of questions. They understand consistency of message, and the importance of the customer experience both before and after a sale is completed.

Success in this role means:

  • Aggressively setting (and beating) monthly goals related to the marketing & sales funnel
  • Creating dashboards and analytic reports for all goals, and proactively communicating all successes and failures to the rest of the team
  • Understanding how our website and product pages drive traffic and leads, and pre-emptively moving to do more of this
  • Figuring out how to make onboarding as simple as possible for our new customers
  • Staying one step ahead of our department's needs by proactively brainstorming new initiatives
  • Growing as a marketer, and developing the capacity to contribute to other areas of our business

Responsibilities:

  • Creating and maintaining a cache of Sales Collateral that articluates the value of RentJuice succintly, usable both electronically and on printed materials (for trade shows)
  • Crafting copy for our website and landing pages, and testing to make sure we're converting as many visitors as possible
  • Developing "Quick Start" guides and other helpful materials that allow RentJuice's newest customers and users to get up and running quickly
  • Working with our Product Team to send e-mail notifications related to our latest product launches and feature updates

What we're looking for:

  • Digital natives who can immedately count off their five favorite companies when it comes to product marketing
  • An understanding of Google Analytics, HubSpot, and other marketing analytics
  • Exceptional writing skills -- meaning brevity and clarity. We'll check your cover letter for this.
  • Photoshop/Illustrator experience isn't required, but is an added bonus that we'd love to know about
  • A track record of striving for continual personal and professional improvement
