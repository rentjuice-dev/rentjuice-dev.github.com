QA Engineer

Location: San Francisco, CA Department: Development Type: Full Time Min. Experience: Mid Level

Send resumes to: 94837-CS-5429@zillow.hrmdirect.com

Zillow, a vibrant growth-oriented company, recently acquired RentJuice to drive growth on the rental side of the business. In addition to a competitive salary, we also offer great stock options, benefits and the opportunity to work with a lot of fun, talented people in the heart of San Francisco.

The QA Engineering position interacts closely with software developers, product managers, and operations to ensure a quality product. This person is responsible for testing software enhancements, documenting software defects, as well as updating and maintaining automated test suites.

Requirements:

  • Strong skills testing web applications and troubleshooting test plans, test cases, and scripts
  • Knowledge of Java (python?)
  • Knowledge of Git
  • Experience with Selenium or WebDriver
  • Experience with continuous integration servers, Jenkins preferably
  • Experience with development and maintenance of automation test suites
  • Strong automated, functional, and regression testing background
  • Ability to multitask and prioritize
  • Excellent communication skills

What you would do:

  • Design, develop, and execute test cases, both automated and manual, for new features and existing code
  • Ensure all issues are entered, tracked, and updated
  • Triage and follow up on incoming tickets from operations
  • Experiment with and recommend new technologies to accomplish any of the above goals

Bonus skills:

  • Mobile Testing Experience

If you love breaking code, chasing down bugs and solving interesting problems, we think you'd love working with us!
