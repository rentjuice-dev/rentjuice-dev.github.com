Social Media Manager

Location: San Francisco, CA Department: Marketing Type: Full Time Min. Experience: Entry Level

The RentJuice Marketing Team exists to increase the overall reach of our brand and generate qualified leads for our Inside Sales Team. When it comes to creating engaging online presences within the overall real estate community, our team turns to our Social Media Manager. The Social Media Manager is tasked with bringing measurable improvements to what is traditionally a very "soft" marketing channel. They're always setting new goals, developing the metrics to track our progress against them, and planning two steps ahead to make sure our growth never slows.

Success in this role means:

  • Aggressively setting (and beating) monthly goals related to followers, likes, reach, virality, and leads
  • Creating dashboards and analytic reports for all goals, and proactively communicating all successes and failures to the rest of the team
  • Increasing our overall marketing reach, allowing RentJuice to reach more eyeballs each day
  • Staying one step ahead of our department's needs by proactively brainstorming new initiatives
  • Integrating social media into other marketing channels
  • Growing as a marketer, and developing the capacity to contribute to other areas of our business

Responsibilities:

  • Creating a social media plan that grows over time with specific monthly and annual goals
  • Testing additional advertising and growth opportunities within social media
  • Interacting with our community and referring any questions to the appropriate department
  • Creating metrics and reports as needed to analyze performance of initiatives
  • Coordinating social media publishing along with our short and long-term initiatives
  • Responsibilities will increase as our organization continues to scale

What we're looking for:

  • Digital natives with personal and professional experience on Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook, and other portals
  • An understanding of Google Analytics, HubSpot, and other marketing analytics
  • A knowledge of social media, the blogosphere, and what makes for viral/compelling content
  • A track record of striving for continual personal and professional improvement
  • A general understanding of RentJuice's mission and software
