Content Marketer

Location: San Francisco, CA Department: Marketing Type: Full Time Min. Experience: Entry Level

Send resumes to: 94167-CS-5429@zillow.hrmdirect.com

The RentJuice Marketing Team exists to increase the overall reach of our brand, and generate qualified leads for our Inside Sales Team. From writing content for The Rental Standard to dreaming up our next viral infographic, a Content Marketer is always pushing themselves to create content that will be valuable to both RentJuice's customers and industry members alike. As our content is consumed and feedback comes in, the Content Marketer is able to gather all applicable analytics to make recommendations on what kinds of material will be most beneficial for RentJuice's business objectives and for professionals who visit our site. The Content Marketer should always be thinking "bigger" when it comes to how we can increase the number of real estate professionals we can reach with our valuable content.

Success in this role means:

  • Aggressively setting (and beating) monthly goals related to traffic, leads, links, and other brand metrics
  • Creating dashboards and analytic reports to reflect on all goals, and proactively communicating all successes and failures.
  • Staying one step ahead of our department's content needs by proactively brainstorming new content ideas
  • Increasing our overall marketing reach, allowing RentJuice to reach more eyeballs each day
  • Discovering ways to leverage our content within our sales and onboarding processes
  • Growing as an overall marketer, and learning additional areas of the business

Responsibilities:

  • Generating content on a fixed schedule, and meeting deadlines regardless of impediments
  • Contacting interested parties to find additional placement for RentJuice's content
  • Conducting market research and surveys in order to uncover interesting data for our articles and e-books
  • Analyzing the performance of RentJuice content across applicable marketing metrics
  • Creating additional metrics and reports as needed to measure new content types and initiatives
  • Owning part of the Content Calendar, and structuring our schedule to meet short and long-term goals
  • Responsibilities will increase as our organization continues to scale

What we're looking for:

  • Digital natives with built-out presences on Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook, and other portals
  • An understanding of with Google Analytics, HubSpot, and other marketing analytical tools
  • An experienced content creator who has written for a blog, newspaper, or other widely-read publication
  • Someone who shows a track record of striving to improve themselves as a marketer
  • A general understanding of RentJuice's mission and software
