What's it like working at RentJuice?

In short, it’s amazing. Recently, we were acquired by Zillow, the online real estate marketplace. While our office will always remain in San Francisco, we've now got the best of both worlds. A strong startup culture with the opportunity to contribute to growing one of real estate's most popular businesses. We're both growing fast and neither of us have plans to slow down anytime soon.


Here at RentJuice HQ, we believe in an open work environment, where every idea is valued, everyone is encouraged to participate, and management is closely involved in building a close-knit and inspired community. At RentJuice, we are constantly trying to find ways to make work more fun. Whether it is playing tug-o-war with the office dog Summer, or participating in a company round of Fantasy Football, we understand the importance of creating a laid back work environment.

Professional Development

We believe that actively training and mentoring our team members is crucial to developing a dynamic workforce. At RentJuice we will provide you with the necessary tools to cultivate your skills and enhance your career path.

Casual Dress Code

We know you want to be comfortable at work and understand that you don’t have to wear a suit to be productive. So, kick-back, relax, and feel free to lounge on the company couch while completing your latest project.


Open Floor Plan

We believe that an open floor plan motivates and creates opportunities for learning. Communication is key, and sitting with your team and management fosters openness and idea-sharing.

Company Happy Hours

Have fun and unwind at monthly company sponsored happy hours after a hard day’s work! Get to know your coworkers better, mingle with management, and toast to new company milestones while throwing back a cold one with the RentJuice team.


Weekly Wednesday Free Lunch

Every week, RentJuice provides free lunch for the team. During this time all employees are encouraged to present and share ideas. This process brings different functions together and promotes team building, while chowing down on some company paid lunch!

Free Snacks

Sustenance throughout the day is important for productivity. Need a snack after lunch? Head to our well stocked company kitchen and choose from our selection of healthy refreshments.
